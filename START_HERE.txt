⚙️  OPTIONAL: ADD REAL WEATHER DATA
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The app works immediately with demo data, but for REAL
weather from your searched city:

1. Get FREE key: https://openweathermap.org/api
2. Open "script.js" file
3. Find line 4: WEATHER_API_KEY: 'YOUR_API_KEY_HERE'
4. Replace 'YOUR_API_KEY_HERE' with your key
5. Save & refresh browser

NOTE: Air quality data works without any key! ✅
